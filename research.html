A Predictive Personalization Approach to Enhance Foreign Language Learning and Teaching

About the Study

This research project explores innovative, personalized approaches to foreign language learning and teaching, aiming to enhance students' learning experiences and improve instructional effectiveness. Funded by the Youth Collaborative Research Grant (YCRG), this longitudinal study was conducted to analyze how students learn a third language - German or French.

What to Expect

Testing Details: The study will assess your language learning abilities through various tests, including:

  • Language learning ability tests across multiple dimensions, including pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, and pragmatics
  • Language learning motivation questionnaire
  • Cognitive ability tests, including both verbal and non-verbal intelligence assessments
